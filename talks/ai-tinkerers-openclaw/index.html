I Fed My Instagram Archive to OpenClaw and Now It Knows I Love Tiramisu

Python PostgreSQL OpenAI ActivityStreams 2 OpenClaw

The obvious way to personalise OpenClaw — give it access to your accounts — trades convenience for a privacy mess. So instead: download your data export, run it through an open-source pipeline, and give OpenClaw the result. We walked through each stage live — the raw archive, the prompts that distil thousands of posts into dated first-person memories, the generated profile, and what happens when you ask OpenClaw to "book a restaurant for me" with all of that loaded.
